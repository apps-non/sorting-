Privacy Policy for Sorting++

Effective Date: May 30, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Sorting++. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines our approach to privacy and the measures we take to safeguard your information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

Sorting++ does not collect any type of personal or non-personal information. We do not require or store any data such as names, addresses, email addresses, phone numbers, payment information, or usage data.

The only input we take from users is the array to be sorted. This input is used solely for the purpose of performing the sorting algorithm and is not stored or shared.

How We Use Information

The array input provided by users is used exclusively for sorting according to the selected algorithm. No other information is used, collected, or stored by Sorting++.

Sharing and Disclosure

Sorting++ does not share, sell, or disclose any information to third parties because we do not collect any personal or non-personal information.

Security

We take reasonable measures to ensure that the array input provided by users is processed securely. However, please be aware that no security measures are perfect or impenetrable.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. The changes are effective immediately after they are posted.
